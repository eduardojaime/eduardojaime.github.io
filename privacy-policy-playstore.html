Privacy Declaration for Spanish Lucky Coin

Introduction

We, the developers, take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Declaration applies to the Spanish Lucky Coin mobile application available on the Google App Store. Herein, we outline the types of information that are not collected from users and our stance on privacy.

Information We Do Not Collect

Spanish Lucky Coin is designed to respect your privacy. As such, our app does not collect, store, use, or share any personal information, browsing history, or any data related to the usage of the application. Our app does not require internet access, ensuring your usage remains private and secure.

Contact Us

Changes to Our Privacy Declaration

We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Declaration at any time. Such changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated declaration on Spanish Lucky Coin. We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.

Effective Date
